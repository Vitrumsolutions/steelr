# Pitch Email Templates

Three templates for three editorial formats. All follow the same rules: short, specific, no hard sell, one clear offer.

Always personalise the first paragraph with a reference to a recent article the publication has run. Editors see hundreds of generic pitches a week; the ones they respond to are the ones that prove the pitcher has actually read the magazine.

House-style reminders: no em dashes, no exclamations, no "best prices", no pricing mentions.

---

## After sending: follow-up protocol

**Day 0:** Send the pitch.
**Day 7 if no reply:** Send a 3-line follow-up: "Circling back on the [topic] piece I pitched last week. Happy to adjust angle or length if useful. Draft still available."
**Day 14 if no reply:** Assume no. Move on to next target. Do not chase further.

Editors appreciate persistence up to one polite follow-up. Three follow-ups = spam.
