# SteelR Guest-Post Outreach Package

Built 22 Apr 2026. Purpose: earn 3–5 published articles on high-authority UK sites over the next 90 days, each containing a contextual link back to steelr.co.uk. This lifts:

1. **Google organic rankings** — every link from a DA 50+ site passes authority to steelr.co.uk. Three links of this quality can shift category-level rankings by 10–20 positions over 60–90 days.
2. **Perplexity discovery** — Perplexity weights third-party citations heavily. Currently cites us by brand, misses us on generic. Three press mentions closes that gap.
3. **ChatGPT reinforcement** — we already rank #1 on ChatGPT for the flagship category query. More citations make that position defensible.

## What's in this folder

- `targets.md` — 10 ranked publications with editor-contact detail and pitch angles
- `pitch-email-templates.md` — 3 reusable email templates (expert contribution, case study, thought piece)
- `article-specifying-steel-front-doors.md` — flagship 1,500-word draft, ready to send
- `article-sr3-vs-sr4-homeowner-guide.md` — security-angle draft
- `article-steel-doors-period-property.md` — conservation / renovation draft
- `outreach-tracker.csv` — spreadsheet of pitches: who, when, status, response

## Realistic expectations

- **Hit rate**: expect 2–3 placements from 10 pitches. Editors are busy; no response usually means no.
- **Time to publish**: 4–12 weeks from pitch accepted to article live.
- **Link lift**: one DA 60+ backlink typically moves a page 5–15 positions in Google over 60 days.
- **Perplexity lift**: once 3+ publications mention us, expect Perplexity to surface us for generic category queries (currently misses us).

## What this is NOT

- Not PR in the expensive sense — these are expert contributions and case studies, not paid features.
- Not guaranteed placements — editors make their own calls.
- Not Claude auto-sending emails — I cannot email from your account. I draft, you send.
