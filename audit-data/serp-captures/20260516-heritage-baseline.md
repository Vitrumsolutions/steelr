## Intent split

Heritage queries fork into two intents Google treats differently:

| Intent | Example query | Winners | Format |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Editorial / planning** | `steel front door grade ii listed` | JLE Studio, Smith Heritage Surveyors, GOV.UK, conservation-officer content | Long-form advisor content with LBC guidance, restrictive AI Overview |
| **Product shopping** | `heritage steel front doors uk` | Latham's, Vufold, DirectDoors, Express Doors Direct, Solidor | Product cards with displayed prices, sponsored shopping carousel dominant |

A single `/heritage-steel-front-doors-uk` page can't dominate both. The brand-aligned bet is **editorial-led with deep spec coverage**, ceding the shopping-intent results to the displayed-price competitors. Same trade-off as `/steel-front-door-vs-composite`.
