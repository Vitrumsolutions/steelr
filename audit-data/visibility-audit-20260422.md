reviews to rank — Google weights reviews heavier than any other Maps signal.

## What's driving the AI wins

1. **llms.txt + llms-full.txt** — engineered for AI crawlers to find structured
   product + certification info
2. **Topic hub pages** (/sr3, /pas-24, /secured-by-design, /fire-rated-fd30,
   /thermally-broken, /uk-vs-imported, etc.) — each one ranks for long-tail
   specifier queries that AI engines surface
3. **FAQ schema** across topic hubs — LLMs weight FAQ-schema-backed answers
   more heavily
4. **Specific technical copy** (SR3 baseline, SR4 LPS 1175, FD30S, Approved
   Document Q, 200 RAL, Chief Bob) — LLMs cite specificity over vague marketing

## Priority gaps (in order of leverage)

### 🔴 1. Zero reviews on GMB — blocks Maps entirely

SteelR GMB has 0 reviews per CLAUDE.md (confirmed in today's audit — no
ratingCount visible). Until the first 5-10 reviews land, SteelR is invisible
on Maps for every query including brand. User manages reviews; flagging as
metric to watch, not prescribing process.

### 🔴 2. Bing post-migration lag — will self-heal

0/15 Bing visibility. IndexNow wired 19 Apr (301 URLs). Expected recovery
mid-late May. Re-check then.

### 🟡 3. Generic category queries owned by Latham's

Every "steel doors UK" / "steel front door UK" / "steel security doors
residential UK" / "steel vs composite doors" query returns lathamssteeldoors.co.uk
in position #1. They have older domain authority, more backlinks, more content.
Hard to displace without years of work — but **specific specifier queries**
(SR3, PAS 24, bespoke + area) are where we can win. Focus efforts there.

### 🟡 4. Area page titles may be under-optimised for premium towns

"steel doors Surrey" has prestigesteeldoors.co.uk at #1, SteelR not in top 30.
But "steel doors Cobham" (Surrey town) gets SteelR at #9. Worth auditing
area page titles/H1s for each premium Surrey + London town to improve.
